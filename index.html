Heaven Eleven

I photographed the iconic East Vancouver 7-ELEVEN store at Kingsway & Joyce in the summer of 2022 after the inevitable development permit sign went up. At the start of March 2023, the windows were boarded up and all signs removed. A week or so later, the famed builder of the global 7-ELEVEN brand, Masatoshi Ito, passed away in Japan at age 98.

This perfectly corner-angled hub of slurpees and hotdogs, social hangout and heatwave refuge, has gone dark forever.

Farewell Kingsway konbini.
